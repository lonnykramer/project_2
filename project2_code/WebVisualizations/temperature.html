Latitude vs Temperature


This scatter plot shows the reported temperatures for the cities. The data shows higher temperature levels close to the equator and cooler temperatures as the latitude increases toward the poles (ie - latitudes increasing in +/- values). Some of the most extreme temperatures are observed in desert regions, such as the Sahara Desert at around 23 degrees North and Tanami Desert at -20 South.
